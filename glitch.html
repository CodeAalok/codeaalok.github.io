Fifty Five DSL

Tweet to Shoot

On the 20th of October, Diesel decided to take Pune by storm.To launch their new line of Street wear, they decided to try something new. A gun, wired up to the internet was set up in the middle of a mall in front of a moving target. Passers by were encouraged to tweet @fiftyfivedsl along with #GetHit, The tweet would trigger the gun and anyone who hit the target would win 55DSL goodies. People thronged to the gun over 3 days resulting in some astronomical outcomes.
