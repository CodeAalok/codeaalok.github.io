Conclusions

  1. Money Power
    Rich candidates scored higher percentage of votes in Chatra and on National level
  2. Party Dominance
    Candidates with party back-up got higher percentage of votes in Chatra and on National level
  3. Criminal Records
    Several candidates have criminal background and a thick percent of these criminals win the elections.
  4. Less Woman Participation
    Higher turn-out for women, but still less or no women contesting elections
  5. Dispersed Leadership
    Lack of coordinated Opposition became a great help to the winner
  6. National Trends
    National situation affects the voter's decision-making in an election
